What is this all about?

Copenhagen Cocoa is the longest running community for iOS* developers in Denmark. We meet for weekly user group meet-ups at Café Retro.

Sometimes we want to spend more than an evening together, that is why Copenhagen Cocoa's Developers Conference is taking place this year, from the 29th of April to the 1st of May.

*iPod Touch, iPhone, and iPad.

What is going to happen?

CCDC 2011 will give participants a unique opportunity to team up with new people, who are passionate about developing and designing apps for Apple's popular iOS platform.

Friday we will kick-off the event by announcing this years theme. The theme will set constraints for the apps developed during the weekend.

When kick-off is over, it is your turn to team up with some people around an concept, and start production as soon as possible.

Sunday 14:00 is the dead-line. Each team will get 5 minutes to present their app. After all the presentations have finished, the winner will be found through a popular vote.

Who can attend?

Anyone can attend CCDC, but if you attend as a participant, we recommend that you have some practical experience within programming or design.
